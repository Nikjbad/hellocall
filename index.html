⚡ Real Features

Everything packed in, all working

HelloCall uses real WebRTC technology — the same tech powering Google Meet & WhatsApp calls.

📹
Real HD Video Calls
Browser-to-browser WebRTC calls with no app download. Crystal clear up to 1080p based on your connection speed.
💬
Live In-Call Chat
Send messages while on a call. Chat history stays for the duration of the call. Works even if camera is off.
🖥️
Real Screen Sharing
Share your entire screen, a window, or a browser tab with one click. Works in Chrome, Edge, and Firefox.
🔒
Peer-to-Peer Encrypted
All video, audio & chat goes directly device-to-device. No server stores your call content. Private by design.

Instant Share Link
One-click copy your room link or share directly on WhatsApp. Anyone can join — no account, no download needed.

Works on Any Device
